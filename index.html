Features and Services

What is Virtualis?

Virtualis is a software that converts movies and shows into VR compatible files. This means you could watch your favorite movie or show through the perspective of any character within the movie!

Our features:

Virtualis has a vast collection of compatible
movies and shows.

Virtualis allows you to watch
sports directly from the point of
view of the player.

Virtualis can be combined with
body suits for extra realism and
a unique experience

About the company

The company was founded in 2019 by a group of five software engineers from the University of Ottawa They started off in the city of Ottawa with an office based inside one of the member's garage but eventually growed to its current mutli million dollar organization.

Our goal

This company's goal is to help the VR market prosper and allow it to spread into other sectors instead of restricting itself only to videogames. With this we could properly utilize VR technology and truly unleash it's vast potential.
